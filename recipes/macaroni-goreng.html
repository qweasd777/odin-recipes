Macaroni Goreng

Description

A Malay-style Macaroni Goreng, aka spicy stir-fried macaroni pasta.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Macaroni
  • 50ml of Pasta Water
  • 2-3 tbsps Dried Chilli Paste
  • 1/2 Onion or Shallots, diced
  • 2-3 Garlic, minced
  • 150g Chicken, diced
  • 2 tbsps Tomato Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Oyster Sauce
  • 1 tbsp Dark Sweet Soy Sauce or Kicap Manis
  • 1 Egg
  • 50g Capsicum (optional)
  • 50g Cherry Tomatoes (optional)
  • 50g Bok Choy (or any leafy greens of choice)
  • Sugar and Salt to taste, if needed
  • A handful of Cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat a pot of water to a rolling boil, and season with salt. Add macaroni or pasta of choice and cook 1 minute less than what is indicated on the packet. For macaroni pasta, the cook time is 8 minutes, so set the timer at 7 minutes. Once timer ends, take the pot off the heat and drain the water away. Keep some of the pasta water.
  2. To a pot add oil and heat over low to medium heat. Once hot, add the dried chilli paste, onions and garlic. Stir fry over low to medium heat until fragrant.
  3. Once fragrant, add chicken, or protein of choice. Add tomato ketchup, oyster sauce and the kicap manis. Stir to incorporate and let the chicken cook.
  4. Once chicken or protein of choice is cooked, add egg. Scramble and stir in with the rest of the ingredients. If using capsicums and tomatoes, add as well.
  5. Add cooked macaroni pasta and 2-3 tbsps of the pasta water to help stir it easier.
  6. Toss in leafy green of choice and stir to incorporate. Taste test, and add salt or sugar if needed.
  7. Finally, toss in a handful of cilantro or your fresh herb of choice. Serve!
